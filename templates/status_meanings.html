Status Meanings

Status Description
Not Contacted The person has not been contacted yet and is awaiting an initial outreach.
In Progress Contact has been made, and follow-up actions or further engagement is underway.
Completed - Interested The conversation has been completed, and the individual has expressed interest in proceeding further.
Appointment Set An appointment has been successfully scheduled for further discussion or engagement.
Completed - Follow-Up Needed The discussion has ended, but a follow-up is required for additional steps or clarifications.
Voicemail Left A voicemail was left as the individual did not answer the call, awaiting a response.
Call Back Later The individual requested to be called back at a later time.
No Answer No one answered the call, and no further action has been taken yet.
Wrong Number The contact number is incorrect, preventing successful communication.
Do Not Call The individual has requested not to be contacted again in the future.
Completed - Not Interested The conversation is complete, and the individual has indicated no interest in further engagement.
Disconnected Number The phone number is disconnected and no longer in service.
